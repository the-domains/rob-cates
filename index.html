About

Robert M. Cates BMus(Hons) MA PGCE QTS Fellow of the College of Teachers, Chartered London Teacher

Rob works as a singer-songwriter, ESTILL trained vocal coach, worship leader and vocalist in the UK and Europe. He currently divides his time between his private and university teaching practices in singing and voice; choral & orchestral direction, coaching and workshops; speaking, preaching and leading worship at churches, conferences and events including at his home church, Catch the Fire London. Rob works across the genres of rock & pop, musical theatre, RnB, soul, gospel and contemporary Christian worship music.
